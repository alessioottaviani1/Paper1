\subsection{Fixed Income Arbitrage and the Limits to Arbitrage}
\label{sec:lit_arb}

% Duffie (2010), Brunnermeier \& Pedersen (2009), Mitchell \& Pulvino (2012).
% Fleckenstein et al.\ (2014): TIPS basis disappeared in US, persists in Italy.
% Hull et al.\ (2004), Longstaff et al.\ (2005), Bai \& Collin-Dufresne (2019).
% Junge \& Trolle (2015), Boyarchenko et al.\ (2016).
% Duarte et al.\ (2007): first systematic analysis, limitations.

The TIPS--Treasury basis documented by \citet{fleckenstein2014tips} averaged
60 bps and peaked at 200 bps during the 2008--2009 crisis
(Figure~\ref{fig:tips_pre2009}). Since then, the basis has effectively
vanished (Figure~\ref{fig:tips_today}), compressed by quantitative easing
and broader institutional participation. In contrast, the Italian
inflation-linked basis on BTP~Italia persists to date---motivating our
focus on the European market.
